Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We pride ourselves on our quick response times. You'll typically receive an initial response within 1 hour during business hours, and within 4 hours outside of business hours. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line.

What information should I include in my travel inquiry?

For the most accurate consultation, please include: your desired destination(s), travel dates, number of travelers, budget range, travel style preferences (luxury, adventure, relaxation, etc.), and any special requirements or interests.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer virtual consultations via Zoom, Google Meet, or phone call. You can book a virtual consultation through our online booking system or by calling our office. Sessions typically last 30-60 minutes.

What are your office hours in different time zones?

We have offices in New York (EST), London (GMT), Dubai (GST), Singapore (SGT), and Sydney (AEST). Each office operates from 9 AM to 6 PM local time, Monday through Friday. Our 24/7 emergency support line is always available for urgent travel assistance.

How can I schedule an in-person consultation?

You can schedule an in-person consultation at any of our global offices by calling our booking line or using the "Book Appointment" button on this page. Please specify which office location you'd prefer when booking.
